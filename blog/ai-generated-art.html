AI Generated Art — A New Way to Create Art

With , from Trevor

Welcome to the future!

Dynamically creating art based on a simple sentance seems like it could be too good to be true, but recently I've been experimenting with midjourney.com, and that reality is today!

I have always struggled to find images online that work nicely with my projects, are aesthetically pleasing, and not filled with watermarks (or free to use). Luckily, I've found a new way to create art that works in seconds and is totally free (at least for the first 30 images). Try it out!

Midjourney is one of a few AI image generators, alongside DALL-E and Google’s Imagen, which are currently in various stages of heavily publicized beta testing. These AI are fed a prompt, and then generate a series of images based upon the prompt and a machine learning algorithm modeled on other pieces of art. - Vice.com
